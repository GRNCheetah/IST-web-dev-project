Europa - Jupiters Ocean Moon

Facts About Europa

  • Eropa is the sixth largest moon in our solar system and is the sixth closest moon of Jupiter.
  • It was discovered in 1610 by Galileo Galilei and named after mother of king Minos and lover of Zeus or jupiter as he is know in latin.
  • This is the smoothest object in our know solar system and it is thought that the reason Europa is smooth is because it houses an ocean beneath its surface of ice.
  • The cause of the dark and distinctive striations running across Europa are seperations of the ice crust on the surface where old ice cracks appart and new ice is formed.
  • Due to the presence of water Europa is a strong candidate for human colinization and its oceans can be inhabited by life, although there has not been any life found on the moon yet.
