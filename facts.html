Moon Facts

What is the Moon?
Our moon is a large rock that is believed to be a piece of Earth that flew into space when Earth was hit by a huge asteroid! It is our largest natural sattelite.
We always see the same side of the Moon
Since the Moon is in a synchronous rotation with the Earth, we always see the same side as it passes over head.
The Moon controls our tides
The Moon's large mass and proximity to Earth allows the Moon to effect our tides. As the moon travels around the world, the ocean is pulled towards the Moon causing a high tide on the side closest to and farthest away from the Moon.
The Moon is 242,815 miles from the Earth
That's a lot of football fields!
