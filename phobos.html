Phobos

  • Phobos is Mars' moon. It was discovered on Aug. 1877 by Asaph Hall.
  • Phobos is the largest of Mars' two moons.
  • It orbits Mars' three times per day
  • NASA says that Phobos is getting closer to mars and that in 50 million years it will crash into mars or have broken into peices.
