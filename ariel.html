Ariel - The Fourth Largest Moon of Uranus

Facts about Ariel

  • "Ariel was discovered Oct. 24, 1851 by William Lassell"
  • "All of Uranus' larger moons, including Ariel, are thought to consist mostly of roughly equal amounts of water ice and silicate rock."
  • "Ariel's surface appears to be the youngest of all the moons of Uranus."
  • "Ariel has the brightest surface of the five largest Uranian moons."
  • These facts were provided by: nasa.gov
